Online busy mode

By enabling the busy mode, you can stop the orders that come in through online platforms such as Mobile Apps / Websites. When it gets very crowded and you can't keep up with the orders coming in, you can also pause the store temporarily. Keep in mind that not all channels except Online delivery Apps/Websites support busy mode.
